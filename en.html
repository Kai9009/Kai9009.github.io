About Me

Kai Zhang is currently an associate professor at the Department of Information Security, College of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai University of Electric Power, Shanghai, China. Previously, he received Ph.D degree in East China Normal University (2012-2017) under the supervision of Prof. Jie Chen and Prof. Haifeng Qian; and was a research assistant in Nanyang Technological University (2017) under the supervision of Prof. Huaxiong Wang.

His research interest include applied cryptography and information security, he has published over 50 paper in referred journals and international conferences, such as IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security、IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing、IEEE Internet of Things Journal、Theoretical Computer Science、The Computer Journal and ACM AsiaCCS、ICICS、ACISP . His work was supported by National Natural Science Foundation of China, Shanghai Rising-Star Program, Shanghai Municipal Education Commission ,State Grid Shanghai Municipal Electronic Power Company, et al.

Research Interests
